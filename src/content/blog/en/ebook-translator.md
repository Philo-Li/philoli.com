I've always made it a habit to read original English books. But honestly, encountering obscure vocabulary or complex sentence structures often diminishes the reading experience significantly. Existing translation tools on the market either only translate webpages, offer questionable translation quality, or their designs are overly complicated with too many unnecessary features.

That's why I created **Ebook Translator**, an ebook translation tool that runs entirely in your browser. Simply paste your own API key to start using it. It supports all major LLM providers and even custom endpoints.

Tool address: [philoli.com/projects/ebook-translator](https://philoli.com/projects/ebook-translator)

<!--more-->

Since its launch, the tool has received widespread acclaim. Its core features include:

-   Translate any book, with translated text displayed alongside the original, supporting over 40 language pairs.
-   Carefully crafted with three popular translation styles: General Natural (close to the original), Fiction, and Professional Books, offering exceptional translation quality.
-   Includes support for mathematical formulas within books.
-   Supports both EPUB and PDF formats.
-   Also supports scanned PDFs, utilizing the powerful multimodal capabilities of AI models to accurately recognize formulas, which works remarkably well even for older books.
-   For specialized books, users can upload their own glossaries or term bases to achieve more precise translations in specific professional fields.
-   Translation progress is automatically saved locally in your browser. If you close the page and reopen it, you can pick up exactly where you left off. Translated books are also stored in your history, allowing you to reopen, edit, or export them at any time. (Supports translation history for the 10 most recent books).
-   Your files are never uploaded to any server; all file parsing and translation requests are processed entirely within your browser.
-   Embracing the design philosophy of "keeping complexity to the software, and simplicity to the user," the interface is clean and elegant, the usage is straightforward, and the features are robust enough to have garnered a great deal of positive feedback already.

## Benefits of Bilingual Reading

On a side note, let's talk about the benefits of bilingual reading.

1.  **Faster Reading.** Many books aren't compelling enough to warrant reading in their original language, or perhaps the author's writing style is simply poor. In such cases, reading the original only adds unnecessary difficulty; it's only with well-written or exceptional texts that reading in the original truly becomes an enjoyable experience. Furthermore, Chinese is a high-information-density language. A million-word original book, when translated into Chinese, might only be equivalent to 700,000 to 800,000 words. For avid readers, this efficiency boost is incredibly significant.

2.  **Improved Language Skills.** Moreover, bilingual comparative reading can enhance your reading speed and proficiency in any language, depending on how you utilize it. This applies not just to English, but to any language. You can read the original version first, and then, upon encountering unfamiliar words, find their translations in the parallel text. The advantage over using a dictionary or built-in dictionary is that it's faster and integrated directly into the text, helping you recognize words more naturally over time.

3.  **Expand Your Reading Horizons.** It's like suddenly being able to read books in any language worldwide, rather than being limited to just a few you already know. Your scope for acquiring information vastly expands. Beyond German, Japanese, or French books, you can now delve into literature and magazines in various less common languages.

4.  **Overcome Initial Barriers.** When language proficiency isn't yet sufficient for rapid original-text reading, a stumbling experience can easily dampen one's interest. An ebook translation tool can help overcome these initial hurdles, fostering a love for reading. Engaging, high-quality books provide positive reinforcement that encourages continuous exploration of the unknown, rather than getting stuck on difficulties and obstacles. Curiosity always comes first; protect your curiosity, and building upon that, you can continuously refine your abilities.

## How to Use

1.  Open [Ebook Translator](https://philoli.com/projects/ebook-translator)
2.  Select your AI service provider and enter your API Key.
3.  Set your source and target languages.
4.  Upload your EPUB or PDF file.
5.  Click a chapter to start translating, or use "Translate All Remaining" for bulk translation.
6.  Once translated, the book will automatically download in EPUB format, or you can manually click "Download EPUB".
